About

I’m a quantitative strategist focused on high-frequency trading and market microstructure. At Mathisys Advisors (2018–present) I’ve traded equities and futures across Brazil, India, and Canada; built real-time quality indicators that improved predictor correlations by 30%+; and modeled intraday volatility for market-making strategies. Previously I worked in Fixed Income & Currencies at Deutsche Bank and held roles at Citi and Deloitte. I hold a dual B.Tech + M.Tech in Electrical Engineering from IIT Bombay, with double minors in Computer Science and Operations Research (AIR 189).

Highlights: 4 research papers (Statistics/ML/IR), Academic Colour Award, UG Research Award, RuSSIR “Most Original Idea”.
